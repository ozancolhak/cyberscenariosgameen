About the Project

"Cyber Security Simulations" is an educational platform that brings real-life cyber crisis scenarios to life through interactive decision-making. Users take on various roles in 8 different scenarios, making strategic decisions and experiencing digital security processes firsthand. This project is developed to increase cyber awareness in educational institutions and companies.

Use Cases

Schools, universities, and companies providing training can use these simulations for staff training, awareness programs, and crisis management exercises.

Technologies Used

Developed as a simple and performant web application based on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It offers gamification techniques and decision-focused interactive experience.
